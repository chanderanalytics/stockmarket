## Notes

- **Wide tables**: `companies_with_price_features*` (360-377 cols), `merged_*_probabilities_wide` (397-429 cols) are generated by R scripts.
- **Price storage**: `prices_bhavcopy_2` (10.6M rows, 4.1 GB) is the primary price table.
- **Symbol mapping**: `companies.nse_code` maps to frontend `symbol`. `companies_with_price_features_with_corp_action_flags.company_code` also contains NSE codes.
- **No `trading_signals` table**: Signals are currently computed in the frontend runtime or can be derived from `vw_trading_opportunities`.
- **No `portfolio` table**: Portfolio data comes from `trade_details` (trade journal).
